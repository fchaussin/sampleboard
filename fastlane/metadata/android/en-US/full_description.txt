Audio Sample Board is a board of sound-triggering pads (soundboard style), deliberately simple: no DSP, no complex audio editing.

Features:

* Multiple pages of pads, resizable grid per page (2×2 up to 12×6)
* Three play modes per pad, using MIDI controller terminology: One-Shot, Gate, Loop
* Per-page polyphony: Mono (one voice at a time) or Poly (layered voices)
* Sample library: import your own audio files (wav, mp3, ogg, flac…), re-encoded to OGG/Opus
* Per-pad gain (in dB), preview, renaming
* Configurable background behavior: stop everything, stop sustained sounds, or keep playing
* Configuration fully persisted and reloaded on startup

The app ships empty: no sounds are bundled, you import your own.

Fully offline: no network permission, no account, no trackers, no ads.
